About

I am Bibek Thapa and I am passionate about technology. I am fascinated and curious about various aspect of technology such as interconnected data, responsive website, realistic games and beautiful VFX. Thus, to have the ability to create something, I decided to become a developer.

I graduate from university of East London studying Computing for Business. I choose dual course because I also had an interest in Business due to various part time jobs in retail industry. However, during the course I really enjoyed the IT part much more than the business part. As the course was mixture between two field, I felt I didn’t have necessary skills and knowledge to be a developer.

So, to get necessary skill and knowledge I enrolled with Just IT, where I am training to be a Web developer. I was trained on HTML, CSS, JS, C#, BS, ASP.NET. I have created many projects where I am trying use the skills and knowledges I learned in a practical application. I am also trying to learn new skills through Pluralsight and other online contents.
